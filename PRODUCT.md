# Prestige Detailing UAE — Product Context

## Product Purpose
Premium automotive detailing studio landing page for Prestige Detailing, located in Dubai Investment Park 2 (DIP 2), Dubai, UAE. The site's job is to convert high-net-worth vehicle owners into booked appointments via WhatsApp or phone. Design IS the product — this is a brand register site.

## Register
brand

## Users
- Dubai's private supercar and luxury vehicle collectors
- Discerning daily drivers who value precision over speed
- Expats and residents who want honest, transparent automotive care
- Corporate fleet owners

## Brand Personality
- Surgical precision without cold sterility — there's warmth in the craftsmanship
- Honest, transparent — owner Mr. Ayman personally supervises every job
- Elite but not arrogant — "treats your car like their own"
- Dubai-rooted with international standards

## Tone
Confident, expert, concise. Technical enough to signal credibility, never condescending. No hype superlatives. Every word earns its place.

## Accent Color
Gold — `oklch(85% 0.170 88)` — used for primary CTAs, active states, and key data points only. Not decoration.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS landing pages (metric cards everywhere)
- Glossy car-wash chain sites with rainbow gradients
- Dark-mode tool dashboards
- Heavy glassmorphism as aesthetic
- Anything that looks like it was generated in 60 seconds

## Strategic Principles
- Trust through specificity: real review quotes, real owner name, real location
- WhatsApp as the primary conversion action (Dubai market norm)
- Mobile-first — high percentage of UAE traffic is mobile
- Every section should feel earned, not templated
